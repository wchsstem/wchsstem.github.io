Club Statement

The STEM club at Winston Churchill High School seeks to foster intrigue and interest in STEM related fields, along with providing for students a catalyst and a community so they can make cool stuff.

We allow interest to be sparked and cultivated to students at Winston Churchill High School. We:

  • Run intro programs/classes
  • Actively involve new members

Along with beginners and novices, we allow students who already possess a burning passion for STEM related affairs by providing them with a community to truly prosper and grow.

About STEM Club

STEM Club is a school club at Winston Churchill High School in Potomac, MD.

About FTC

FTC is a robotics competition run by FIRST to inspire young people in middle school and high school to take an intesrest STEM fields. For the competition, teams of students build and program a robot that performs tasks autonomously and with driver control.
