Club Statement

The STEM club at Winston Churchill High School seeks to foster intrigue and interest in STEM related fields, along with providing for students a catalyst and a community so they can make cool stuff.

To advance our goals, we:

  • Run intro programs/classes
  • Actively involve new members
  • Along with beginners and novices, allow students who already have pre-existing expertise in STEM related affairs by providing them with a community to truly prosper and grow.
  • Partake in hackathons/CTFs/other STEM-related events
  • Initiate and complete projects either of club or member origin

We seek to thoroughly explore and implement our understanding of STEM from beyond the classroom, beyond paper, and beyond solely theory. By engaging students-moreover, anyone who aspires to reach the bounds of STEM- we hope that we can equip our members with knowledge and experience extensively, whether up to professional grade or to that of a professed hobbyist.

About STEM Club

STEM Club is a school club at Winston Churchill High School in Potomac, MD.

About FTC

FTC is a robotics competition run by FIRST to inspire young people in middle school and high school to take an interest STEM fields. For the competition, teams of students build and program a robot that performs tasks autonomously and with driver control.
